📚 A1 Beginner Level Study Plan

Based on the students' scores in the placement test, it was found that they are currently at the lower end of the A1 (Beginner) level. This study plan is designed to reinforce fundamental language skills and help build a strong foundation in English. The focus will be on basic grammar, vocabulary, and practical communication, with emphasis on simple sentence structures and everyday situations.

The students will study for 2 hours a day, three days a week, for a month. The plan will focus on 6 key units from the New Headway Beginner course, followed by a final review and test on all covered content.

📅 3 days per week
